# Strong Fit Example

This example shows the shape of a strong STRIDE recommendation.

## Role Fit Summary

Strong fit. The role's core needs align with the candidate's demonstrated architecture, platform engineering, cloud modernization, stakeholder leadership, and operational readiness experience.

## 10-Second Hiring Manager Scan

The candidate should read as a credible staff-level platform and cloud systems engineer. The top of the resume should immediately surface platform architecture, distributed systems, cloud modernization, security-conscious delivery, and cross-functional technical leadership.

## ATS and Semantic Alignment Matrix

| Requirement | Evidence | Strength | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Cloud architecture | Source resume shows cloud-native and hybrid-cloud architecture | Strong | Use role-specific cloud terms where truthful |
| Platform engineering | Source resume shows internal platform and modernization work | Strong | Make this prominent in summary |
| Executive communication | Source resume shows stakeholder and executive-facing communication | Strong | Keep concise and concrete |
| Compliance-aware delivery | Source resume shows regulated delivery context | Moderate/Strong | Avoid unsupported compliance ownership claims |

## Recommendation

Apply.

This is a credible fit if the tailored resume makes the staff-level architecture and delivery story clear within the first scan.
